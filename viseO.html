About Me

My name is Stefan Kovačević, I am 23 years old, born on July 23, 2001. My journey into programming started in elementary school, but it truly blossomed when I enrolled in the Information Technology department at "Rade Končar" Technical School in Belgrade. There, I quickly developed a passion for programming, fascinated by its logic, creativity, and problem-solving aspects. After high school, I continued my studies at the Faculty of Information Technologies (ITS) in Belgrade, where I deepened my knowledge in languages like C, Java, PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C++. I learned advanced programming concepts, including Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), and gained practical experience by working on dynamic web applications and animations. I also completed an internship at the National Bank of Serbia, where I worked on programming-related projects, giving me insight into how technology functions in large organizations. In addition to my academic experience, I’ve worked on personal projects, creating custom web applications, and I completed an IT Mentorship program to further refine my skills. Passionate about technology and problem-solving, I’m always seeking new challenges. My goal is to develop innovative solutions to help businesses and individuals succeed in the digital world, and I believe my skills and dedication would make me a valuable asset to any team.
