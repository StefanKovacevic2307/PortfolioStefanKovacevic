Highlighted Projects

Here I showcase two important projects that highlight my skills in web development and design.

Parket Store Project

This project represents an innovative platform for selling parquet flooring and providing professional craftsmen services. Through a modern and functional design, we have enabled users to explore a wide range of parquet options, select the perfect materials for their home, and contact experienced parquet professionals for advice or engagement. The platform was developed using PHP, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, ensuring optimal performance and an intuitive user experience. Special attention was given to integrating craftsmen's work showcases, service reviews, and detailed product descriptions. This project not only simplifies the purchasing process but also enhances interaction between customers and service providers. Through this system, we promote transparency, quality, and efficiency in the parquet industry.

Artist Gallery Project

This project is an online gallery showcasing the work of a renowned Serbian artist, offering art enthusiasts the opportunity to explore the richness of his creations. The gallery is thoughtfully designed to display numerous pieces of the artist's work, each with detailed descriptions and pricing. Through a simple and intuitive design, visitors can easily browse the gallery, learn more about each artwork, and make a purchase decision. The platform uses technologies such as PHP, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to ensure quick access to all information and easy navigation through the gallery. Special emphasis has been placed on high-quality image displays and the straightforward presentation of prices and details for each piece. This project not only allows visitors to enjoy the art but also enables them to directly purchase the artist's works, contributing to the expansion of his artistic presence and strengthening his market reach.
